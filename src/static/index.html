How It Works

1. Upload Before & After Images

Upload images of your server rack from different points in time. Our system accepts standard image formats and works with various rack types and configurations.

  • Front and rear rack views
  • Close-up or full rack images
  • Various lighting conditions

2. AI-Powered Change Detection

Our advanced AI algorithms analyze the images to detect hardware changes, including:

  • Added or removed equipment
  • Relocated hardware
  • Configuration changes
  • Cable modifications

Changes are highlighted visually and detailed with confidence scores and location information.

3. Reconciliation with Asset Management

The system compares detected changes with your asset management records to identify discrepancies:

  • Unregistered additions or removals
  • Misplaced equipment
  • Configuration inconsistencies
  • Documentation gaps

Detailed reports help you maintain accurate inventory and improve compliance.
